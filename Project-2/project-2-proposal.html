Element Drainer

HIGH CONCEPT: Element Drainer is a resource collecting game in which you must collect pockets of different elemental energies from the ground so that you can continue through the levels and make it to the treasure room at the end of the game.

GENRE: Survival, Arcade, Round-Based

PLATFORM: PC

STORY: The player plays as an adventurer that has been trapped in The Cave of the Ancients. The Ancients were beings of magical powers that had control over 4 elemental types of magic: Fire, Wind, Water, and Stone. Your goal is to collect pockets of Elemental Energy with the draining device you inherited from your third cousin. You can dig through the ground with this device to reach the energy youâ€™ll need. After enough is collected, the Ancients will be appeased and allow you to the next chamber. Watch out for traps and negative energy.

ESTHETICS: The art style of this game will be basic pixel art, similar to the popular indie game Undertale. The sounds of this game will be 8-bit style. There will be different sound effects for each element type when you pick the energy up, there will be a fanfare when you complete a level, and there will be ambient creepy music playing during the level.

GAMEPLAY: This game will consist of 3 main mechanics: movement (WASD), digging (mouse click and arrow keys to change direction), and collision detection for traps and blocks being dug out. There are 4 different kinds of the elemental energy and the player must collect the right amount of each kind before the time runs out. If the player steps onto a trap or collects a pocket of negative energy (denoted by a purple hue), they will lose progress towards their end goal.
